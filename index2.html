About

Established in 1999 by Namrata Neupane, affectionately known as Namy, IT Chai Wali is a cozy nook that bloomed from a daring decision. Namrata's journey, marked by her departure from the 5th semester of BIT, embodies the courage and passion that fuels IT Chai Wali to this day.

At IT Chai Wali, we offer a delightful array of breakfast and biscuits paired with a diverse chai menu. Our masala chai, infused with aromatic spices, warms the soul, while our milai chai promises a creamy indulgence. For those seeking tradition, our matka chai brings a rustic charm to every sip, and the boldness of black chai satisfies the most ardent chai enthusiasts.

We also cater to refreshing variations like lemon chai, perfect for a zesty twist, and ginger chai for a comforting, spicy kick. Amidst the aroma of freshly brewed chai and delectable bites, IT Chai Wali offers a unique ambiance where patrons can also immerse themselves in a selection of captivating books. Whether you're seeking a stimulating read or a tranquil chai experience, IT Chai Wali welcomes you to indulge in a blend of flavors and stories that ignite the senses and nourish the mind.

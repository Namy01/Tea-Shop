Details About IT chai-wali

Once upon a time, in a land not so far away, I began my academic journey with dreams as grand as the size of the chai cups at my favorite tea stall. Little did I know, the chai wala would become my unintentional career counselor. One fine day, I strutted into Bhaktapur Multiple Campus, dreams of academic glory shimmering in my eyes. However, reality hit harder than a spicy chai when I saw the fee structure of BIT. It seemed like they mistook me for a distant relative of the royal family! As if that wasn’t enough, the questions they asked in exams felt like they were plucked from a parallel universe, definitely not from our syllabus. So, what did I do? Did I gracefully exit? Nope! I took a drop — not from the rooftop, mind you, although that might have been less painful! And then, in a stroke of genius, I started selling chai. Yes, you heard it right! I figured if I couldn’t afford the campus fees, I might as well start my own tea empire. Who knew that my chai-wala instincts were stronger than my academic prowess?

Little did I know, the owner of this chai wala, where I found solace from academic woes, turned out to be none other than my dear friend, Namy Poor She.
